About University

DDU Gorakhpur University is a leading state university in Uttar Pradesh offering a wide range of UG, PG, and research programs. The university has a legacy of academic excellence and commitment to societal growth.

Courses Offered

  • Postgraduate: MA, MSc, MCom, MCA
  • Professional: B.Ed, M.Ed
  • Doctoral: Ph.D. in various disciplines
